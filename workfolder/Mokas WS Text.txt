-- [ ENGLISH ] --

As the release of ARC9 has come, so has quite a few weapon packs, so I present to you: Gunsmith Reloaded, or GSR for short!

Taking inspiration from the infamous and controversial yet beloved Gunsmith Offensive addon, Gunsmith Reloaded utilises the power of the mighty ARC9 by adding many features and finally introducing new features, such as functional Akimbo weapons, functional UBGL's and even more attachments than before!

Join the official [url=https://discord.gg/gaHXusZ]Diamond Doves Discord server![/url]

[h1]FAQ[/h1]
- [b]How do I customize?[/b] - Just like on ArcCW, you press your Context button (default 'C')
- [b]How do I melee?[/b] - Hold your USE (default 'E') button and press your primary attack (default 'Left Mouse')
- [b]Will it add more weapons later?[/b] - Maybe.
- [b]Will it receive more attachments later?[/b] - Only time will tell.
- [b]What do I need to use this?[/b] - All you need is the ARC9 base.

[h1]FAQ[/h1]
- [b]Hur anpassar jag?[/b] - Precis som på ArcCW så trycker du på Context-knappen (standard 'C')
- [b]Hur använder jag närstridsanfall?[/b] - Håll inne din ANVÄND (standard 'E') knapp och tryck på din primäreld (standard 'Vänster Mus')
- [b]Kommer fler vapen läggas till senare?[/b] - Kanske.
- [b]Kommer den motta fler tillbehör senare?[/b] - Får vänta och se.
- [b]Vad behöver jag för att använda detta?[/b] - Allt du behöver är ARC9-basen.
